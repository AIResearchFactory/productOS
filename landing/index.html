Open Source · Local-First

End context switching with ProductOS,
your all-in-one local AI workspace

ProductOS is a free, open-source desktop workspace built for product managers. It runs entirely on your machine — no cloud, no subscriptions — and connects to any AI model you already use (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, or local Ollama). ProductOS organizes your research, documents, and AI conversations by project, so your AI always has the right context. Create PRDs, run competitive research, build automated workflows, and go from raw notes to polished artifacts — all in one private, local workspace.
