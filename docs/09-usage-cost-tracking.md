## Overview

Working with large-scale AI research can become expensive if not managed correctly. **productOS** includes several built-in tools to help you track your AI usage, understand your costs, and optimize your token consumption.

---

## Token Saver: Smart History Optimization

**Token Saver** is a powerful feature designed to significantly reduce your API costs without losing context during long research sessions.

### How It Works

As your AI conversations grow longer, the entire history is usually sent to the LLM with every new message. This increases your costs exponentially.

When **Token Saver** is enabled:
1. **Preserves Recent Turns**: It keeps the most recent 6-8 conversation turns exactly as they are (ensuring the AI remembers your immediate requests).
2. **Compacts Old History**: It summarizes older parts of the conversation into a single, dense context message.
3. **Reduces Input Size**: This creates a much smaller payload for the API, saving you money and keeping the AI focused.

### Enabling Token Saver

You can toggle this feature directly from the AI chat interface:
1. Open the Chat Panel (💬).
2. Look for the **"Token Saver"** icon (a small shield or coin icon) in the header.
3. Toggle it **ON** to start saving tokens immediately.

### Why Use It?

- **Save Money**: Reduce your API bills by 30-70% on long conversations.
- **Stay Under Limits**: Help prevent hitting provider context window limits.
- **Better Focus**: By summarizing old context, the AI stays focused on your current task rather than getting distracted by old, irrelevant parts of the chat.

## Usage Tracking

**productOS** tracks the number of tokens used for every request, whether it's via chat, a skill, or a workflow.

### Viewing Your Usage

You can see a summary of your usage in:
1. **Global Settings → Usage**: View total tokens consumed across all projects.
2. **Project Dashboard**: See usage specific to your current research initiative.

### Metrics Captured

- **Input Tokens**: Tokens sent to the AI (prompts, context, history).
- **Output Tokens**: Tokens generated by the AI (responses, research results).
- **Saved Tokens**: Tokens that were *not* sent thanks to **Token Saver**.
- **Estimated Cost**: A rough calculation based on standard market rates for the models you are using.

## Best Practices for Cost Management

### 1. Match the Model to the Task
Don't use top-tier models (like Claude 3 Opus or GPT-4o) for simple tasks.
- Use **Claude 3 Haiku** or **Gemini Flash** for research and summarization.
- Use **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** or **GPT-4o** for complex analysis or PRD generation.

### 2. Leverage Local Models
For basic research, brainstorming, or testing workflows, use **Ollama** via MCP. This is completely free and runs on your own hardware.

### 3. Use Workflows Wisely
Workflows process items in parallel, which can consume tokens quickly.
- Test your workflow with 1-2 items first to verify the output quality before running it on a list of 50+ items.

### 4. Keep Token Saver ON
Unless you are doing extremely sensitive work where every historical word is critical, keep **Token Saver** enabled to maintain efficiency.
